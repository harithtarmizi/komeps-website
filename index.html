BACKGROUND

Why Choose Us

KOMEPS FinTech began as KOMEPS FinTech PLT (LLP0009526-LGN), a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) company registered in Malaysia since 2016. KOMEPS FinTech SDN. BHD. soon after was established in 2019 as an SPV to undertake the “D-8P Card” project partnering with the D-8 Organization for Economic Cooperation (D-8 Organization). The company hails proudly at past achievements of the company’s leadership.

One of KOMEPS FinTech’s directors was a consultant to Gemalto, providing secure transaction advisory and market intelligence inputs. The public listed company he founded in the early 2000s successfully supplied a quantum amount of 50 million Gemalto smart cards to the Malaysian banks and at the same time securing the banking industry’s ATM pin-pad transactions.

Under the auspices of the economic bloc D-8 Organization, KOMEPS FinTech is undertaking a massive global payment system project, to be implemented across the D-8 Member States (Malaysia, Indonesia, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Iran, Turkey, Egypt and Nigeria).
